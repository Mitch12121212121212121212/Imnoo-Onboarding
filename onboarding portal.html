Settings for the first Time Estimation

Set up your account for a first time estimation — add machines, configure manufacturing steps, and prepare for estimations.

Click to play (2:18)
2:18
Step-by-step guide:
1.Go to Settings and select Machines to begin adding your machine.
2.Choose a machine brand & model. If not listed, add a new one and specify the type (e.g. Milling, Turning).
3.Enter max article length, width, height and weight capacity for manufacturability matching.
4.Click Save after entering all machine details.
5.Navigate to Internal Manufacturing Steps — add steps like milling, turning, grinding, etc.
6.If applicable, add a Turn-Milling step combining both technologies.
7.Select the machining rate for calculations — pulled from your machine list.
8.Save your settings — you're ready for your first time estimation!
